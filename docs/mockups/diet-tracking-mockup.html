UX rationale (what to react to):
• Entry from scans: the lime “＋ Add to today” sits directly under the score on the existing result screen — no new step to reach it.
• The new home base: a dedicated “Today” tab in the bottom nav (Home · Explore · Today · History) is the daily destination. Open question below.
• One “Add food” button, three inputs (packaged scan / meal snap / manual) so there’s a single mental model for logging.
• Trust (quietly): meal snaps pre-fill name + portion + macros, and always give an editable name + adjustable portion before they hit your totals — the correction step is there without ever saying “AI guessed this.”
• Targets: smart defaults; the ⚙︎ on Today opens overrides.
